Equipment


Tools

There are a variety of tools you start with in Terraria that will help aid you with fighting, mining, and exploring the dangerous land. You will be able to upgrade your equipment as you explore, mine, craft, and gain more items.

Starting Equipment

As you start the game you will begin with only three items.
A Copper Pickaxe, Axe, and Heavy Shortsword




  • You use the pickaxe to mine your sorroundings. Remember though, your pick axe has mining power so you might not be able to mine certain ores and blocks until you craft a better pickaxe with the sorrounding ore you can mine.
  • Your axe is similar to the pickaxe, however it only cuts down trees, cacti, and other varients of that nature.
  • Your sword, and other weapon types, are used to take down the enemies the will spawn around you. Different weapons have unique fighting styles try to branch out when crafting and use different weapon types to find you preferred play style. Each weapon type branches out into different classes Melee, Ranged, Mage, and Summoner.


    • These will be the items that will help aid you in the start of your journey. With wood you obtained with your axe, ore you mined with your pickaxe, and loot dropped by the enemies you slayed you'll be able to craft a crafting table and start your journey to being a better player!
