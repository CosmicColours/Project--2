Progression

As you explore around the world you might encounter bosses, boss spawners, and even events that act like raids of enemies coming to attack you. It might seem scary and they might appear when your not prepared, most often they appear at night. But, it's in your best interest to beat them as they most often either drop very good items upon death that let you become stronger, or unlock more parts of the world and game.
